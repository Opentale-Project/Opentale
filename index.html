What is OpenTale?

OpenTale is a future community-driven, open-source game inspired by the spirit of Hytale. Dive into a voxel-based sandbox world where creativity, adventure and modding possibilities are endless.

🧱 Block-based World

Explore and shape vast worlds built from beautiful, modifiable blocks.

⚔️ Adventure

Fight, build, craft and survive in an epic fantasy universe.

⚡ Powered by community

Powered by the passionate (and impatient) Hytale community!

🧑‍💻 Open Source

Everything is transparent and hosted on GitHub. Help us build the future.
